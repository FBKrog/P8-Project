What is the Arm Bazooka Project?

A VR project where you can detach your arm from your body by firing it. When it hits a surface, it'll stick, and you can continue using your arm at that location.
The aim is to see how well this would allow for distant object manipulation, when compared to more traditional methods such as a laser.
